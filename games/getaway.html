Getaway

Ever wanted to break the rules? In Getaway, you can do so!
There are 2 teams made of 5 players each. One team is robbers, and the second is police. The main goal of the game is not just to eliminate opponents. You and your team also need to steal (or reclaim) valuable goods.
DISCLAIMER: The game is currently very early in development and isn't subject to release shortly.
